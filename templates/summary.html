Can I convert this?

Shorter Yardage = Higher Odds. 4th & 1 is likely to convert, but 4th & 9 isn’t so easy.

Can I trust my kicker?

Even if you are in range, is your kicker consistent? Is the weather affecting things?

How many chances will I get after this?

How much time is left? Do I have timeouts? If not, this might be your last shot.

What happens if I fail?

Field position matters. A failure could be the difference between handing your opponent the ball at the 20-yard line or the 70-yard line.
